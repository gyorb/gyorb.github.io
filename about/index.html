Who am I?

Hi, I’m Gyorgy Orban and this is my personal blog. I love to create software and I’m a full time software developer. I’ve worked on multiple telecom related projects over the years. Currently I’m involved in the open source development of the CodeChecker project.

When not coding, I’m usually having fun outdoors or go to the gym.

I love technology, in this blog I will write about projects I work on or tech I find useful or interesting.
